Portfolio Project

SmartComputersKE – Social Media & Website Management

Client: SmartComputersKE Technologies

Role: Digital Marketing Associate

Platforms Used: Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Company Website

Tools: Canva, CapCut, Meta Business Suite, WordPress

Overview

SmartComputersKE is a tech company specializing in computers, laptops, and accessories. I played a key role in enhancing both their social media presence and website content to increase online visibility, customer engagement, and sales.

My Contributions

  • Social Media Marketing
    • Created and scheduled posts promoting daily deals, new arrivals, and tech tips.
    • Designed Reels and TikToks featuring product demos, unboxings, and customer offers using CapCut.
    • Increased Instagram engagement by 45% and reached over 2,000 TikTok views within the first month.
  • Website Management
    • Updated product listings, images, and banners on their WordPress-based website.
    • Wrote SEO-friendly product descriptions with keywords like “affordable laptops in Kenya” and “best printers Nairobi.”
    • Collaborated with the sales team to ensure accurate pricing and descriptions.
    • Added landing page banners for monthly promotions using Canva and optimized mobile display layouts.

Results

  • Boosted web traffic by approximately 20% during the promotion months.
  • Helped improve the bounce rate by simplifying product page layouts and improving mobile responsiveness.
  • Contributed to a more cohesive online brand look across platforms.
